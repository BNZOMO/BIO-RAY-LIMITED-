Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service? ▼

We primarily serve Nairobi and surrounding areas in Kenya. Our team can travel to various locations for consultations, installations, and maintenance. Contact us to discuss your specific location.

How much does installation cost? ▼

Costs vary depending on the scope of your project. We provide free consultations and transparent quotes with no hidden charges. Contact us to schedule a site assessment.

How long does installation take? ▼

Installation timelines depend on the project scope. Simple systems can be installed within 1-2 days, while complex integrated systems may take 1-2 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.

Do you offer maintenance and support? ▼

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance packages including scheduled check-ups, emergency support, and spare parts replacement. We provide 24/7 support for urgent issues.

Are your systems compatible with mobile devices? ▼

Most of our modern systems include mobile app integration for remote monitoring and control. CCTV systems, access control, and smart home systems can be accessed from your smartphone or tablet.
