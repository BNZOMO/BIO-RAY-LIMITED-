About Bio Ray Solutions

Bio Ray Solutions is a Kenya-based provider of integrated security, fire protection, and solar energy solutions. We deliver smart, reliable systems including CCTV, alarms, access control, electric fencing, fire safety, smart home automation, and solar installations—ensuring safety, efficiency, and peace of mind.

Mission

To provide sustainable, reliable and innovative security, fire, and solar solutions with integrity and professionalism.

Vision

To be a trusted global leader in security, safety, and sustainable energy solutions.
