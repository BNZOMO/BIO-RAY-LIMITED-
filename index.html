About BR Shield Solutions

BR Shield Solutions is a Kenya-based provider of integrated security, fire protection, and solar energy solutions. Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service? ▼

We primarily serve Nairobi and surrounding areas. However, we can arrange installations and maintenance services across Kenya for larger projects. Contact us for details about service availability in your location.

How quickly can you install a system? ▼

Installation timelines depend on the project scope. Simple systems can be installed within 1-2 days, while complex integrated systems may take 1-2 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the consultation phase.

Do you offer maintenance contracts? ▼

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance packages tailored to your system's needs. Regular maintenance ensures optimal performance and extends equipment lifespan. We provide scheduled check-ups, emergency support, and spare parts replacement.

Are your systems compatible with mobile devices? ▼

Most of our modern systems include mobile app integration for remote monitoring and control. CCTV systems, access control, and automated home systems can be accessed and managed from your smartphone, tablet, or computer.

What's included in the initial consultation? ▼

Our consultation includes a thorough site assessment, security audit, system recommendations, cost estimates, and a detailed implementation plan. There's no obligation, and we're here to answer all your questions.

Do you offer warranties? ▼

Yes, all our installations come with manufacturer warranties on equipment and our service guarantee on workmanship. We also offer extended warranty packages for added peace of mind.
